Principal's Desk

Mrs.Punam Sharma

Started as a small dream to be realized with a few hundred students the school has made tremendous growth to the level of Higher Secondary School housing more than two thousand students with marvelous infrastructure and the best facilities available.
The school was started with the vision of changing and transforming the prevalent educational scenano Our main aim has been to impart education using innovative methods of teaching aimed at the development of the cognitive domain from the facts to skills in order to mould students as complete human beings for better prospects of their future.
Discipline and value education aimed at enhancing moral development of students has been yet another very crucial aspect of the school.
I'm confident that our students wil continue to make the optimum use of the opportunities that we offer to them and will emerge as strong individuals who hold their heads high wherever they go.

Vision

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school's respect, integrity, innovation and excellence.

Mission

We ensure that everyone in our community becomes inspired, innovative and passionate about achieving what matters, anywhere in the world. We will inspire academic and personal growth in our students by encouraging inquiry, stimulating creativity, innovation and fostering meaningful participation and service.

Philosophy

We at Divine Educational Institute provide our students with the opportunity and motivate them to develop skills to become lifelong learners, enabling them to take responsibility for their learning and well-being. We recognize the importance of physical and emotional challenges by providing diverse learning, creative, social, active and service opportunities.

Welcome!

Divine Educational Institute,Sikandrabad is one of the leading schools in the National Capital Region.

Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education, the school believes in the Holistic Development of the students. We believe that the youth are the future of this world and on this belief, we try to inculcate the best qualities for the development of the students.

We have a multitude of facilities for the development of the students and certified teachers who are dedicated to developing the students to the fullest and making them the of who they can be. What makes a student a great student are great teachers, awesome facilities and a great vision.
