What We Offer

Safety First

Our school determines and maintains appropriate policies on school safety, welfare, and supervision for all school activities, whether taking place in or outside school premises.

Certified Teachers

Our school has well-certified teachers who have passion and dedication to teaching. They provide students ample amount of facts and knowledge in the best way they can.

Sufficient Classrooms

We have a sufficient number of classrooms to accommodate students so that every child has its space to learn and can be interactive and friendly with others.

Regular Classes

Regular classes are conducted in school to provide a classroom friendly environment to the children and to build up their daily routine.

Co-curricular Activities

A lot of inter-school competitions such as debates,quizzes,dance,music,art and poetry are conducted to boost up the confidence in children from an early age.

Sports Facilities & Courts

Play fields are the heart & soul of an institution,they are the bulwarks of a healthy society. As a matter of fact, our school has rich networks of courts & play fields.

Library

The infrastructure provides a student friendly and aims at developing the reading skills among them. Fiction, non-fiction, encyclopedia, Grolier International, reference books, resource material for students and teachers fill the shelves of this library. Educative and informative leading dailies in English and Kannada and magazines are also made available. They are extensive, exhaustive and boast of the latest reference needs

Computer Lab

Well-equipped Computer labs with all facilities are made access to students to upgrade themselves technologically. The School Computer laboratories foster the development of life-long learning abilities and love for computers through an array of resources – such as a scanner, projector system and a wide collection of CDs on a variety of subjects – to implement, support and enhance these requirements.

Physics Lab

Our Physics Lab is a capacious room which caters to the experimental minds of our students. Lab activities and experiments engage student’s mind which help them to acquire necessary skills, knowledge and confidence. The lab is equipped with the latest equipment as per CBSE curriculum where students carry out their experiments in an innovative and inspiring environment.

Playground

Biology Lab

To provide hands-on practical experience to our students, excellent equipment and facilities are provided for Biology Lab in accordance with the CBSE curriculum.
